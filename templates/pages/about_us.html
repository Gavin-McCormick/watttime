About WattTime



WattTime was founded in 2013 by UC Berkeley economics PhD student Gavin McCormick with a simple vision: empower people to take control of their own energy choices, based on their own values, instead of leaving those choices up to energy companies. With a little data and some clever timing, people can now have a say in their own energy supply like never before.

WattTime is a small but passionate team of economists, software developers, designers, and cleantech professionals based in the San Francisco Bay Area. Between us, we have experience at leading companies and institues throughout the energy sector (including DOE, 3TIER, and the Institute for the Future), and an impressive range of graduate degrees.

If you're excited about our mission and want to help us build some truly novel tools to craft the clean energy economy through timing and energy choice, then please get in touch! We're particularly interested if you are an energy industry professional, front-end or mobile developer, or mechanical engineer.
