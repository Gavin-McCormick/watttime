About Us


Why we're here

Our vision is simple: empower people to control their own energy choices, based on their own values, instead of leaving those choices up to power companies. By combining groundbreaking new techniques in energy economics with emerging sources of real-time grid data, that vision of energy choice is becoming a reality. Now everyone can have a say in the emerging clean energy economy.

Founder Gavin McCormick and technical co-founder Anna Schneider met at the 2013 Berkeley Cleanweb Hackathon and have been having a great time working on WattTime ever since. We love spending every day combining our skills from graduate research at UC Berkeley (Gavin in behavioral and energy economics, Anna in computational biophysics), our professional experience in the energy sector, and our joy in doing big things with teams of talented, dedicated, and friendly folks. I would say that you can also find us in the woods working on our treefort, but you probably couldn't—we'll have to take you there sometime.

WattTime's stellar developers, designers, and buildings engineers may be split between the Bay Area and London, but we're connected by our passion for crafting ever more powerful, easy, and fun ways to use cleaner energy (and by our love of Indian food).

—Anna, Caroline, Colin, Gabe, Sean, and Vish

Energy markets are complex beasts. WattTime's world-class economists and analysts have the technical chops to spend our days wading through the data, and the domain expertise to know when we've discovered a new way to make a real-world difference. Our motto: we can do better.

—Dilek, Gavin, Giovanni, James, Nate, and Patrick

The WattTime community is open source, open data, and open to your contributions—please do get in touch! Now's a particularly exciting time to get involved if you like working on mobile, social, Internet of Things, satellite imaging (yes really), or energy economics.
