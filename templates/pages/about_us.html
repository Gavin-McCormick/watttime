About WattTime



About

WattTime was founded in 2013 by UC Berkeley economist Gavin McCormick with a simple vision: empower people to control their energy choices, based on their own values, instead of leaving those choices up to energy companies. By combining groundbreaking new techniques in energy economics with emerging sources of real-time grid data, that vision of energy choice is becoming a reality. Ordinary people can now have a say in the future of the clean energy economy.
