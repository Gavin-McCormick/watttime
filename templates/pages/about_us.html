About WattTime



WattTime was founded in 2013 by UC Berkeley economics PhD student Gavin McCormick with a simple vision: empower people to take control of their own energy choices, based on their own values, instead of leaving those choices up to the energy companies. By combining groundbreaking new techniques in energy economics with emerging sources of real-time grid data, that vision of energy choice has become a reality. Ordinary people can now have a say in how the electricity sector works like never before.

At WattTime, we spend our days crafting never-before-seen tools to keep making energy choice easier, cheaper, and more empowering. We are constantly pushing the envelope by employing (and contributing to) the newest research in sustainability, behavioral economics, and hardware design.

Want to help? If you share our passion for placing the power of energy choice in people's hands, please get in touch! We are a small but passionate team of economists, software developers, designers, and cleantech professionals based in the San Francisco Bay Area. Particularly if you are an energy industry professional, front-end or mobile developer, cultural entrepreneur, or mechanical engineer, we would love to hear from you.
