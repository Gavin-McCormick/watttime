About Us


How We Got Started

WattTime was founded in 2013 by UC Berkeley economist Gavin McCormick with a simple vision: empower people to control their own energy choices, based on their own values, instead of leaving those choices up to power companies. By combining groundbreaking new techniques in energy economics with emerging sources of real-time grid data, that vision of energy choice is becoming a reality. Now everyone can have a say in the emerging clean energy economy.
