Privacy Policy


WattTime takes privacy seriously. We don't sell, share or publish your individually identifiable information without your explicit consent. Period. When we collect personal information it is only for the purposes of providing our users with better energy intelligence. However, we believe that rigorous scientific research and people-centered design can be an important tool in building collective action against climate change. We may publish any scientifically relevant findings about energy economics, social practices, or service design in journals and blogs if we can do so in a way that will protect your privacy and make the world a better place.
