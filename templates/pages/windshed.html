Network Prowess

To accomplish this feat, Windshed creates the network analytics necessary for leveraging electricity consumers as a "network bridge" between the legacy electricity grid and emerging wind power generation stations. As part of a Renewable Integration Response program, Windshed helps consumers expand their electricity supply options while helping wind farm and electricity grid operators span infrastructure gaps and balance the weak ties of costly and wasteful network congestion with excess wind farm production.
