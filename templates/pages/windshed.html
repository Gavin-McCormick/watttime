Smart Grid Intelligence

Sustainability programs managers often make difficult decisions about where they should invest in incentives for smart grid technology adoption. Demand response devices like thermostats, smart plugs, and electric vehicle chargers can pay for themselves by nudging market demand to take advantage of surplus wind.

Climate Committed

Windshed maps the best locations for smart grid technology adoption and incentive programs by showing where intelligent electricity timing can stimulate high impact economic incentives for wind energy. This has the added co-benefit of reducing CO2 and other greenhouse gasses.

Network Prowess

To accomplish this feat, Windshed creates the network analytics necessary for leveraging electricity consumers as a "network bridge" between the legacy electricity grid and emerging wind power generation stations. As part of a Renewable Integration Response program, Windshed helps consumers make a difference while helping wind farm and electricity grid operators fight costly and wasteful network congestion with excess wind farm production.
