How It Works


WattTime is Built for You

WattTime is designed to support and enhance your lifestyle while fostering a cleaner energy economy.
It’s more than a message. It's a movement.

Empowering

WattTime creates options for you to exert more control over your clean energy choices. Find out what works for you.

Easy and relevant

WattTime is free and easy to use. Getting started is quick, and real-time tools ensure that you get relevant information.

Big impact

A resilient future

Join others and create the clean energy economy. Help build a more resilient future, and use collective action to power the world.
