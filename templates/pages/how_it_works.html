How WattTime Works


Imagine buying food from someone who wouldn't tell you where it came from. The finest local, organic ingredients or old highway roadkill? You might be uncomfortable not knowing what you're getting! And yet for most of us, that’s exactly what buying electricity is like. When you flip a light switch, what are you getting—solar power? Coal? You probably have opinions about what power source you’d prefer.. if you only had a choice.

With WattTime, you can find out what fuel you’re getting. Check out the real-time status of the electricity grid in different regions. Because electricity is traded in real time in markets the size of several states, the real-time cleanliness of electricity tends to be the same across fairly large regions at any given time. But it changes a lot over time! And sometimes it’s unpredictable. The current fuel mix varies with weather including wind, sun, and snowmelt; with the current demand for electricity; with the strategies of different power companies; and so on. So we also offer a text message notification service. We can notify you when your power meets the criteria you ask to be notified about.

And what to do with this information? We spend a lot of time thinking about small, easy ways to make a big impact with your fuel choices. We’ll send you helpful hints with our texts and can personalize them to ways that are convenient for you, yet make a big impact on the issues you care about. Maybe you have air conditioning and want to use more renewable energy? We can let you know on days when cooling your house extra in the morning would mean you could cool more with renewable power. And over time, with your help we can measure our impact ever more precisely and continually improve the quality of our information and its relevance to your life.
