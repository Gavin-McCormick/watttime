How It Works


WattTime helps you choose your power

Care about energy?

If you had a choice, would you care what kind of power fuels our nation? Clean power like solar and wind, or aging fossil plants?

Your voice matters

Every day, hundreds of power plants compete to sell you power. At WattTime, we think it's time you had a say in who wins.

Timing is everything

When you flip a light switch, your money goes to different power plants depending on how the competition is going right now.

We can help

WattTime monitors the grid 24/7 to give you real-time information about who is trying to sell you what, when.

Affect your world

Just by choosing when you want to draw power and when you'd rather wait, you can have a big impact. Finally, you can have a say in what kind of power fuels your lifestyle!
