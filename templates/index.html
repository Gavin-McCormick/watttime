Getting Started is Easy

WattTime lets you know when the electricity you consume is coming from renewable sources.
You decide when and what kind of energy you want to use.

Step 1

Sign up is simple

All we need is your email address and phone number to get you started.

Step 2

Verify your device

We make sure your device is verified so we can send secure notifications directly to your device.

Step 3

Get more notifications

Context matters. With just a few questions about the kind of energy you use at home or work, we can send you more relevant notifications.

Step 4

Trigger clean energy economics

When you receive a text, join others and create the clean energy economy by shifting your consumption patterns to cleaner energy sources.
