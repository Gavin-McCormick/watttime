Getting Started is Easy

WattTime lets you know when the electricity you consume is coming from renewable sources.
You decide when and what kind of energy you want to use.

Step 1

Sign up is simple

All we need is your phone number to get you started. We'll keep you updated about new features and regions if you include your name and email too.

Step 2

Verify your device

Just follow the instructions so we can send notifications directly to your device.

Step 3

Get more relevant notifications

Context matters. With just a few questions about the kind of energy you use in your home or at work, we can make the notifications more relevant for you.

Step 4

Trigger a cleaner energy economy

When you receive a text, join others and create the clean energy economy by shifting your consumption patterns to cleaner fuel sources.
